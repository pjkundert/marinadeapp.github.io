About the App

The Altar app (formerly known as Marinade), is a Bible meditation tool designed to help users memorize and reflect on Bible scriptures. It allows users to save favorite verses from various Bible versions, organize them into categories, add personal notes, and meditate using digital flashcards. Users can also record themselves reading scriptures and listen to the recordings during activities like driving or running. The app supports usage on iPhone, iPad ensuring accessibility across multiple devices. With a user-friendly interface and features aimed at enhancing personal engagement with the Bible, this app serves as a valuable resource for those seeking to deepen their spiritual practice.
